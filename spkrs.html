Speakers

Nitin Sood :co-founder HappyShappy

Born in a small town in Punjab, Nitin grew up at a boarding school in the hills and then left for the US to pursue higher education. He earned a Masters degree in Computer Science from The George Washington University, and joined one of the fastest growing technology consulting firms in DC during the second year of his Masters program. Over the following decade, Nitin built a solid career in technology. He spent several years working with the US government, and then at the World Bank. Nitin developed high-tech solutions to complex issues in education, labor and development. He led cross-functional teams with his technical expertise in enterprise architecture , big data and artificial intelligence.
