ART & CRAFT COMPETITION

How you draw is a reflection of how you feel about the world. You're not capturing it, you're interpreting it.

KARTAVYA 2020 presents CANVAS, an Art & craft competition which provides you a chance to show your painting, sketching and crafting skills.

Converge, create and compete!

Open to college and senior secondary students only.

Themes for Painting and sketching:
-The beauty of Nature
-Cleanliness
-Life in lock down
-Impact of COVID-19

Pick out any of the themes and get ready with your pencils, brushes and colors.

For Crafting: Use waste materials and make best out of that.

Last date to submit: 13th April, 2020

Results declaration: 15th April, 2020

Perks: E-certificates for best 3
