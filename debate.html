Team Debate Competition

"Freedom is hammered out on the anvil of Discussion, Dissent and Debate."

For the love of debating and new experiences, NSS DTU presents to you, 'What's Your Opinion?', its debating event for Kartavya 2020, the one of a kind social e-fest.

Exciting prizes and thought-provoking discussions and debates await you!

Theme: Social Issues.

E- Certificates will be given to the top 2 teams along with a shoutout on NSS DTU's social media handles.

Deadline for registration: 11:59 pm, 14th April 2020

Debate Day: 15th April 2020

Result Declaration: 16th April 2020!!

Rules:
1) The debate will take place in Zoom.
2) The language for the debate in English.
3) Participants may register via the google form latest by 13 April.
4) Please Note: This is a conventional debate ie, participation is allowed only in teams of 3.
5) Only team leader will do the registration.
6) There is a strict team cap of 10, and selection is on a first come first serve basis.
7) All teams are requested to register only once to avoid any confusion.
