Case Study

Take part to solve a given scenario to the best of your analytical abilities in Case Study Challenge by NSS-DTU’s eFest - Kartavya 2020.


Rules & Regulations:
1. The solution must be mailed to nss@dtu.ac.in latest by 12:00 AM, 13th April 2020. Late entries will be penalized or disqualified at the discretion of the organizing team.
2. The introductory page must have the Team Name, Participant Names, Phone Numbers, Email Ids and College Name(s).
3. The solution must be submitted in a PDF Format named “Team leader”. Solutions should not exceed 8 slides (excluding appendix and the title page).
4. Any assumptions made must be clearly stated and justified. Any calculations (if used) should be shown.
5. Use of internet is allowed.
6. Plagiarism will result in disqualification.
7. There is no predefined format in which the case has to be attempted. The participants may use their discretion in this matter.
8. The judgement shall be made based on logical flow of solution, analytical skills, viability of suggested solutions and innovative ideas.
